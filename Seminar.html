Student Reading Seminar in p-adic L-functions and Fontaine rings, 2014 Term 1.


Supervising Professor: Professor Sujatha Ramdorai.

Lectures:
  • Mondays 3:00-4:30 PM, room MATH 126
  • Fridays 10:00-11:30 AM, room MATH 126, starting the week of October 19

Seminar Goal: The aim of this seminar is to study p-adic L-functions over cyclotomic extensions of number fields from the classical work of Iwasawa and then relate it to Fontaine's theory of (φ, Γ)-modules.

Prerequisites: Algebra and Number theory as in Math 501 and 538.

Evaluation: Each registered student is expected to give a few lectures on a topic. Grades will be based on classroom participation and lectures in the seminar.
