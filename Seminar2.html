Seminar Sujatha II: Pro-Unipotent Fundamental Groups and the Theorems of Siegel.


Supervising Professor: Professor Sujatha Ramdorai.

Lectures:
  • Wednesdays 12:30-3:00 PM, PIMS, starting the week of Janurary 19.
  • Wednesdays 12:00-1:00 PM, room MATH 1118;
  • Wednesdays 2:00-3:00 PM, room MATX 1118, starting the week of Janurary 19.

Seminar Goal: The aim of this seminar is to study étale fundamental groups and their Hodge-de Rham theory.

Prerequisites: algebra and number theory as in Math 501 and 538, exposure to algebraic topology and algebraic geometry as in Math 527/528 and 532/533 is not necceary but highly recommanded.

Evaluation: Each registered student is expected to give a few lectures on a topic. Grades will be based on classroom participation and lectures in the seminar.
